ABOUT RAS -

RAS is an International Student Recruitment Organization that assists students in pursuing overseas education in universities in USA, UK,Canada.RAS is a solution-oriented company, which specializes in tailoring career pathways to suit individual profiles within the constraints that may prevail in each case. The constraints may be educational, financial or personal.

SYSTEMATISED APPROACH TO STUDIES ABROAD
